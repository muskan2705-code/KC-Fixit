Who We Are

KC Fixit is a premium construction chemicals brand focused on dependable bonding, site-ready systems, and long-term performance across modern Indian construction requirements.

Our range covers tile adhesives, tile grouts, epoxy systems, waterproofing solutions, ready-mix plasters, block adhesives, and polymer additives so contractors, builders, architects, dealers, and project teams can work with a more complete system instead of isolated products.

The brand is guided by one practical belief: when product quality stays consistent, teams build faster, finish cleaner, and reduce avoidable failures later.
