The substrate needs to be cleaned & made free from grease & loose particles. Then
dampen the surface with clean water.
Add Ready Mix Plaster to potable water
(this will be for 515 mins)
. Recommended mix
1517% water.
Allow Mixture to stand for 5 mins. & then remix again for 2 mins.
Apply plaster to the substrate, using the usual plastering tools (trowel or wooden
float if required).
Apply aluminium level strip from bottom to top direction followed by left to right (let
it drain if required).
Plain plaster wall ready for wall putty application & Post 24 hours curing is
recommended for 7 days. (
