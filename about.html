Who We Are

KC Fixit is a premium construction chemicals brand built for professionals who need dependable bonding, controlled application, and long-term performance across Indian construction conditions.

The range brings together tile fixing solutions, tile joint fillers, epoxy systems, waterproofing products, ready-mix plasters, block adhesives, and polymer support so project teams can choose connected systems instead of isolated materials.

Our approach is simple: stronger product architecture, clearer technical communication, and solutions that stay practical for contractors, builders, architects, engineers, dealers, and project buyers.
