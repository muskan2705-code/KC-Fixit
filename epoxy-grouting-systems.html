Epoxy Grouting Systems

Epoxy grouting systems for wet zones and tougher service conditions.

Epoxy grouting is the stronger choice when ordinary joint filling is not enough. In wet areas, higher-use zones, and maintenance-sensitive environments, the performance demand on tile joints becomes much more serious.

KC Fixit Epoxyshield 2K and 3K are positioned to meet that need with a more premium, performance-led jointing system. They help architects, builders, contractors, and project teams step up from routine grout when the service environment becomes more demanding.

These systems are best suited to projects where durability, upkeep, and stronger joint integrity matter just as much as the tile finish itself.
